Amanda Martin’s Work Life Began in the Banking Industry

Before Amanda Martin decided to become the face of Iron Resurrection, she was once a corporate worker – and worked in the financial industry as a banker. Amanda worked in the banking industry for several years and landed her first job straight out of college.

Her first banking job was as an employee of the American multinational financial company Wells Fargo. She worked with Wells Fargo for four years, beginning in March 2007 and lasting until December 2011. During those four years, Amanda rose in ranks from a simple clerk to becoming the Branch Manager of the bank and later Vice President.

In 2012, Amanda got another job with Pioneer Bank. There, she held a number of different positions, starting from Assistant Vice President to the Deposit Operations Vice President and then a Product Manager. She was still in that position when she decided to quit the industry entirely.

She Left the Bank to Become a Reality TV Star on Her Husband’s Show

Amanda Martin left the financial sector to become part of her husband’s (Joe Martin) business and TV show. Now, she is a co-owner of the automobile refurbishing and customization company Martin Bros Customs, along with her husband.

She also became a television star after their automobile business was chosen by Discovery Channel to be filmed in a reality television show titled Iron Resurrection. Amanda and her husband are co-stars on the show, which has been rated by many as one of the best automobile shows on television.

On the show, Joe, Amanda, and other castmates search the Texas countryside for old, broken-down motorcycles and cars they can fix and make brand new again. Joe, unlike his wife, has been in the automobile business for many years and has been fascinated with cars since childhood.

The first three seasons of the show were an immense success. Martin Bros Customs’ popularity has soared to the extent that they not only earned very handsomely but also owned several branded merchandise, including shirts and baseball caps. There are currently speculations over the return of the show for a fourth installment.
