Emily Maynard Expecting a Baby Boy!

It’s a boy! Emily Maynard revealed she’s going to have a son during an interview with WCCB Charlotte News. The pregnant former Bachelorette, who is already mom to 9-year-old daughter Ricki, teased the gender of her baby-to-be with an Instagram photo of a gender reveal cake two months ago, but never revealed the sex until now.

The 28-year-old jewelry designer, who named her daughter Ricki after her late fiance Ricky Hendrick, said she’s attempting to call the shots again this time with husband Tyler Johnson.

“I told Tyler that [if] he gets to pick the last name for everybody, then I should pick the first name, and if he wanted to pick the first name, then it can take my last name and be a Maynard!” the former Bachelor contestant joked. “He did not like that. We’re still compromising.”

The formerly single mom, who lost Ricki’s dad in a 2004 plane crash, said it’ll be nice to have Johnson, whom she met in church, to coparent with.
