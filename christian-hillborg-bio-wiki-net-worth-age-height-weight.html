Biography

Christian Hillborg is best known as a television actor. Swedish star, known for his work in both film and television. He is best known for his many recurring roles in different shows, including Erik on the 2017 shows The Last Kingdom, Daniel on Bron/Broen, and Ancker on the Hidden: Forstfodd show. He was born in Stockholm , Sweden, on March 16, 1978. He stared alongside Alexander Dreymon and Ian Hart in the 2015 action series The Last Kingdom. He started his acting career in 2006 when he played a small role in the Hombres show. He made his first recurring role debut in 2011 when he starred in Bron / Broen. His feature film debut came the same year he starred in Easy Money.
