Know About Erin Moriarty; Age, The Boys, Movies, Parents, Height, Dating

Not many actors have had a chance to share the screen with some of Hollywood’s greatest like Mel Gibson and Viggo Mortensen. In those few names, Erin Moriarty, age 25, makes her way through.

Erin Moriarty is a gorgeous actress known for her roles on the television series One Life to Live and True Detective. She ringed her acting debut in 2010.

Erin Moriarty Short Bio: Age, Parents, Height

The 25-years-of-age actress was born Erin Moriarty on 24 June 1994. She hails from New York City, the United States. There are no details of her parents or if she has got some siblings. However, she describes her folks as very stylish people.

With a decent height of 5 feet and 6 inches (1.68 m), Erin was passionate about acting since her early age. After she graduated from high school, she deferred college to find her feet in the showbiz.

Career In TV And Movies

The young actress moved to Los Angeles to pursue her career in acting. Soon, she landed a television role of Whitney Bennett in the American soap opera broadcast, One Life to Live, in 2010. A year later, Erin guest-starred in a single episode of Law & Order: Special Victims Unit.

Erin’s debut big-screen project was the 2012 sci-fi comedy flick, The Watch. Later in 2016, she shared a screen with one of the greatest actors, Mel Gibson, in the action-crime-thriller film, Blood Father. Her other big work was with another Hollywood’s finest, Viggo Mortensen, in the 2016 comedy-drama film, Captain Fantastic.

Erin also made a three-episode appearance in the anthology crime-drama series, True Detective, in 2014. She now portrays the role of Annie January / Starlight in the American superhero web television series, The Boys (2019- ).
