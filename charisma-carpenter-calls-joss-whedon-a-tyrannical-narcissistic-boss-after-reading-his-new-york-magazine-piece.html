Charisma Carpenter Calls Joss Whedon A 'Tyrannical Narcissistic Boss' After Reading His 'New York Ma

Charisma Carpenter is responding to Joss Whedon‘s own response over his alleged toxic behavior.

If you didn’t see, Joss spoke with New York Magazine about the allegations against him, which included Charisma‘s own remarks and his words towards her when she alerted him that she was pregnant during the fourth season of Angel.

“[I] did not call her fat,” he claimed. “Most of my experiences with Charisma were delightful and charming.”

After reading the article, Charisma spoke out on social media.

Click inside to read more…

In her response, Charisma utilized a bit of sarcasm to combat his entitlement.

“#IStandWithRayFisher, the ‘malevolent force’ and ‘bad actor in both senses’ who poisoned my feeble mind with trendy buzzwords and corrupt ideas about my experiences with a former tyrannical narcissistic boss who is still unable to be accountable and just apologize,” she wrote.

Additionally, she also stood up for Gal Gadot, who claimed Joss had threatened her career, and he responded that English wasn’t her first language.

To this, Charisma wrote, “I believe Gal Gadot not only understands career threats in English, but also in Hebrew and Arabic. Possibly French, Spanish and Italian too.”
