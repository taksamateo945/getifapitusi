Kylie Jenner Looks Gorgeous In Vivienne Westwood With 300 Carats Of Diamonds From Lorraine Schwartz

After Kylie Jenner stunned in Ralph & Russo at the Vanity Fair Oscar party, she donned a gorgeous red number from Vivienne Westwood for another party. She looked stunning in the rich color and paired the dress with Lorraine Schwartz jewels — 300 carats worth of diamonds to be exact. Kylie shared photos of herself in the outfit with her 162 million Instagram followers where the photos have gone viral. Kylie wore the Cocotte Red Gold Label Silk dress that is not available for sale online. She wore a pair of Louboutin Hot Jeanbi 100 Lace Pumps that retail for approximately $900. Jill Jacobs styled Kylie for her second Oscars after-party that Beyonce and Jay-Z threw.

Kylie looked like a goddess as she posed before arriving at the lavish affair. Her hair was parted off-center and was sleek and had a glossy shine as it fell over her shoulders. Kylie kept her makeup complementary to her red dress and chose light bronze tones for her eyes and a soft, muted brown for her lips.

Most striking was the 300 carat Lorraine Schwartz diamond necklace that Kylie wore. The necklace featured three layers with large, brilliant stones.

Lorraine shared several photos of Kylie along with the following caption.

@kyliejenner is STUNNING 😍💎💋!!! Dripping in over 300 carats of #lorraineschwartz diamonds 💎💎💎💎💎 @jilljacobsstudio

The Vivienne Westwood dress had a beautiful drape and featured a cold shoulder look. The top of the dress had a sheath running across from one arm, across the chest, to the other making it the perfect frame for Kylie’s diamond necklace.

With gentle bunching at the waistline, the dress accentuated Kylie’s incredible hourglass figure and hugged her curves.  Featuring a high slit on the left side, Kylie showed off her toned legs and made a striking impression on her fans who are sharing the pictures. So far, the photos Kylie posted on her Instagram account have over seven million likes.
