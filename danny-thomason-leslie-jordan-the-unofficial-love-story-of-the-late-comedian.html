Danny Thomason Leslie Jordan: The Unofficial Love Story of the Late Comedian

Who was Danny Thomason?

Danny Thomason was the partner of Leslie Jordan, the American actor, comedian, writer, and singer who passed away in October 2022. According to Genius Celebs, Thomason and Jordan were in a loving relationship, but they were not married. Thomason was a younger man who helped Jordan overcome his difficulty accepting compliments.

The details of how Thomason and Jordan met are not publicly known. However, in February 2022, Jordan disclosed to Pride Source that he had a new man in his life who he met online. He also revealed that his partner proposed to him after just three days of meeting in person. Jordan said that he was surprised by the proposal, but he accepted it because he felt a strong connection with Thomason.

What was their relationship like?

Thomason and Jordan had a happy and supportive relationship. They often shared pictures and videos of themselves on social media, showing their affection and humor. Jordan also credited Thomason for helping him cope with the pandemic and his career challenges. Thomason was proud of Jordan’s achievements and encouraged him to pursue his passions.

How did Jordan die?

Jordan died on October 24, 2022, at the age of 67. He apparently had a medical emergency while driving and crashed into a structure. His death was confirmed by TMZ and Variety, who reported that he suffered a heart attack. Jordan’s fans and friends expressed their shock and sadness over his sudden demise, and paid tribute to his legacy as a comedian and a LGBTQ+ icon.

How did Thomason react to Jordan’s death?

Thomason was devastated by Jordan’s death. He posted a heartfelt message on Instagram, saying that he lost his soulmate and his best friend. He also thanked Jordan for the love and laughter they shared, and said that he will always cherish their memories. Thomason asked for privacy and respect during this difficult time, and said that he will honor Jordan’s wishes for his funeral arrangements.
