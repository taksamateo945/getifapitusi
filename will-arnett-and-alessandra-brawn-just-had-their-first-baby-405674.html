Will Arnett And Alessandra Brawn Just Had Their First Baby

A new report from Page Six just revealed that Will Arnett and Alessandra Brawn had their first baby together. A representative for the Bojack Horseman actor said that their son, Alexander Denison Arnett, was born on the 27th of May.

Their spokesperson says their child, Denny, is currently at home with them and everything is going well. Fans of the couple know that the 50-year-old actor and Alessandra have been linked publicly since last year in 2019.

It was revealed they were going to have a child together in February of this year, and an insider who spoke with Us Magazine claimed that both parties were elated at the news. Denny marks Arnett’s third baby thus far.

Currently, Will has sons Abel and Archie, 9 and 11, with his ex, Amy Poehler. He and Amy were married for thirteen years from 2003 until 2016. Back in 2018, Will said he couldn’t be happier about becoming a father.

Bojack Horseman has been hailed for its take on a number of contentious issues, including racism, sexism, addiction, self-destructive behavior, sexuality, and the perils and strengths of the human condition. GQ Magazine described it in the past as the benchmark by which all other comedies should be measured.
