Amy Adams Not Expecting Twins, Golden Globes Speech Sparks Questions

Did Amy Adams just make a huge announcement at the Golden Globes? Not quite. The overjoyed actress celebrated her 2015 Golden Globes win for Best Actress in a Comedy/Musical for her role as Margaret Keane in Tim Burton's Big Eyes on Jan. 11 with a heartfelt speech. 

One line in particular made people question whether the five-time Oscar nominee was actually expecting twins via a surrogate mother. Adams, 40, acknowledged a woman named Megan, saying, "To Megan, at home for nurturing twins in her womb. Sorry, it's getting weird."

Megan Moss Pachon is, in fact, Adams' publicist, who is currently pregnant with twins.
