Michael Strahan's Ex-Wife Jean Muggli Arrested for Allegedly Attacking Ex-Girlfriend

Michael Strahan‘s ex-wife Jean Muggli has been arrested.

Jean, who was married to Michael from 1999 until 2006, was arrested on Friday (June 25) in New York City after she allegedly attacked her ex-girlfriend Marianne Ayer.

Click inside to read more…

According to TMZ, Jean was taken into custody and booked for criminal contempt for violating an order of protection involving Marianne, who claimed she was being terrorized by Jean.

Marianne had filed legal documents earlier this month accusing Jean of “grand larceny, disorderly conduct, harassment, coercion and forcible touching.”

She also claimed in her documents that Jean took her son’s plastic gun, which he was using for a student film, and threatened Marianne, saying that the gun was real.

Marianne also claimed that Jean abused her pets, by allegedly shoving and kicking one of them.

Jean was arrested for allegedly violating the order of protection by showing up at Marianne‘s house.

Together, Jean and Michael share 16-year-old twin daughters Sophia and Isabella.
