Sharon Osbourne to take break from The Talk

Sharon Osbourne is to take a break from The Talk after she collapsed.

The talk show host will take a month off after she collapsed due to “extreme exhaustion”.

A spokesperson for Sharon said she had “collapsed from physical and mental fatigue”.

“Sharon would like to thank everyone at The Talk for granting her this time off and to all friends, family and others for their incredible support”.
