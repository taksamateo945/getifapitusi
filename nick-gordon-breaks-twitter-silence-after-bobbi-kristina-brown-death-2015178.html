Nick Gordon Breaks Twitter Silence After Bobbi Kristina Brown's Death

On Monday, Aug. 17, Nick Gordon tweeted for the first time since the death of his girlfriend, Bobbi Kristina Brown. Though Gordon’s account is private, Extra shared the tweet from the emotional Gordon. 

“I look at our pics sometimes it makes me smile sometime I cry @REALbkBrown #happymemories," he wrote. "I'm so happy/blessed that I had @REALbkBrown in my life. i will always & forever love you."

Brown passed away at age 22 on July 26. Her Georgia funeral was held on Aug. 1, and a second gravesite ceremony was held several days later when Brown was laid to rest next to her late mom, Whitney Houston, in Westfield, N.J.
